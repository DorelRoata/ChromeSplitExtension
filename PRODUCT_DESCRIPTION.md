# Split View for Chrome

## Overview
**Boost your productivity with the ultimate multitasking tool for Chrome.**
Split View allows you to view multiple websites simultaneously within a single browser tab. Whether you're comparing documents, watching a video while working, or monitoring multiple dashboards, Split View organizes your web experience into a clean, resizable grid.

## How to Use
1.  Open the websites you want to view in separate tabs.
2.  Click the **Split View** extension icon.
3.  Select **Dual View** or **Quad View**.
4.  Your tabs will instantly merge into a unified split grid!

## Privacy Assurance
Split View runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, store, or transmit your browsing history or personal data. Use it with complete peace of mind.
